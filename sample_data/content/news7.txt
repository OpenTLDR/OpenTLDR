In dramatic news today, a local man affiliated with a Pro-CARB organization was charged with the unlawful production and distribution of PB&J sandwiches. The sandwiches have long been banned by local establishments due to a combination of peanut allergies and carbohydrate concerns. In recent days, an obsession with PB&J sandwiches has been blamed for multiple incidences in our community, including the arrest of our own Mr. Charles Chuckles.
